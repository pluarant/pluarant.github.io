Real-time Analytics

Mesibo platform allows to collect information from across your App and API usage and monitor in real-time. The main advantage is to monitor real-time where improvements are needed and the usages of messages, voice and video calls.

Security & Encryption

Mesibo messages and calls are end-to-end encrypted. You can even add your own encryption and key exchange mechanism, e.g., Double Ratchet Algorithm.

Filtering & Moderation

Download entire Mesibo platform and run it from your own data-center or private cloud for ultimate control over your user data & privacy. All messages and calls route through your own data-center.

Scalable

Mesibo's carrier-grade platform supports millions of simultaneous connections at low latency and high reliability. You can scale your apps to millions of users from the day one.
